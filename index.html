WELCOME TO LOYOLA SCHOOL HARNAUT

Loyola School, Harnaut is a private School established and conducted by the Catholic Church Harnaut, which is a unit of the Archdiocese of Patna, for the education of boys and girls up to Higher Secondary level. It is open to all without distinction of caste or creed. The purpose of the institution is to impart to its pupils a sound, moral, intellectual and physical education, to train them to be useful and loyal citizens of India and to help them to become agents of needed social change in the society Realizing that education is an all round development of pupil, the institution constantly endeavors to direct to this and co-curricular activities with special emphasis on discipline, formation of character and self reliance. The school follows C.B.S.E Syllabus. The medium of instruction is English. Moral instruction, which is essential for character formation is imparted to all pupils. Sanskrit is taught as a third language from class VI to VIII. Computer Science, Drawing, Art & Craft, Health Education are integral parts of curriculum.
